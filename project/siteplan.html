Site Plan

Logo

Name: Kaluku Musics

Reason: This name represents a music school/ organization that is dedicated to their customers and their need, that is reliable and ready to share of Jesus Christ.

Site Purpose: This site was created as a need for an accessible and reliable service. This website will seek to bring information about a music school and how people can get around to apply for the music school. It will showcase the many programs offered by the school, a sign up form as well as a directory. It would come in handy as people could now access music lessons from professionals online.

Scenarios

1. What type of music classes does Kaluku Musics offer?

2. What is the best package/deal that available for our budget?

Color Schema and Typography

Colors: darkblue, lightblue and yellowgreen. Darkblue for background color, lightblue and yellowgreen for fonts and texts.

Typography: Lora for headings and Edu VIC WA NT Hand Precursive for paragraphs.
